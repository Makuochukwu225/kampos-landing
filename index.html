Awesome Features

  • Academic Resources

    Kampos gives you access to past questions and detailed solutions for previous years, it also comes with course material, course notes from lectures and report and defense examples.

  • Events calender

    Stay up-to-date on campus events and activities with Kampos's events calender. You can find everything from club meetings to social events and more

  • Roommate finder

    Looking for a compatible roommates? Kampos allows you to search for potential roommates based on location, budget, and other preferences.

  • Peer-to-peer marketplace

    Need to sell or buy textbooks, furniture, or other items? Kampos has a built-in marketplace where you can connect with your peers to buy and sell products.
